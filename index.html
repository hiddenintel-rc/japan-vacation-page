Explore Japan

Discover culture, cuisine, and landmarks from Tokyo to Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Located in Kyoto, this Shinto shrine is famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates and is dedicated to Inari, the deity of rice and prosperity. A spiritual and scenic hike awaits visitors.

Ueno Ameyoko Shopping Street

Ameyoko is a bustling open-air market street in Tokyo’s Ueno district. Once a post-WWII black market, it's now a lively strip for food, clothes, and street snacks.

Ichiran Ramen in Ueno

Ichiran offers tonkotsu ramen in solo booths, perfect for immersive, customized flavor experiences in a quirky setting.

Shibuya Crossing

Tokyo's iconic scramble crossing surrounded by bright lights, department stores, and a buzzing youth culture vibe.

Odaiba Bay

A futuristic man-made island district featuring waterfront parks, the Unicorn Gundam statue, and Rainbow Bridge views.

Gundam Base

DiverCity's ultimate Gundam store and museum in Tokyo, with giant models, Gunpla kits, and a life-size moving Unicorn Gundam.

Osaka Ninja History Museum

Explore ninja lore through hands-on exhibits, performances, and stealth tools at this immersive Osaka attraction.

Bullet Train (Shinkansen)

Japan's sleek Shinkansen offers ultra-fast, smooth rides across the country, blending efficiency and elegance in travel.

Namba Yasaka Shrine

Osaka’s shrine with a massive lion-head stage said to swallow evil spirits and bring good luck. A dramatic photo op!

Tsutenkaku Tower

A retro Osaka landmark with panoramic views, quirky exhibits, and the good-luck deity Billiken.

Shinsekai District

A nostalgic Osaka neighborhood full of Showa-era charm, street food, and neon vibes.

Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji)

A Zen temple in Kyoto adorned with gold leaf, reflecting beauty and harmony over a tranquil pond.

Nikko Toshogu Shrine

An ornate UNESCO World Heritage site and mausoleum of shogun Tokugawa Ieyasu, surrounded by forested beauty.

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo’s oldest Buddhist temple with a vibrant shopping street and the iconic Kaminarimon gate.

Kiyomizu-dera Temple

A Kyoto treasure with a large wooden stage and sacred waterfall, offering seasonal views and spiritual depth.

Shiodome District

A modern urban center in Tokyo with skyscrapers, designer shops, and Miyazaki's giant Ghibli clock.

Wagyu Beef

Renowned for marbling and tenderness, Wagyu is a luxurious taste of Japan—best served grilled, seared, or as sushi.
